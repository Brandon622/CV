SMT AFRITECH

We are the largest manufacturer of solder and related soft alloys in Africa. The metals (pg1) used in our products, range from tin, lead, silver, copper, antimony, bismuth, germanium, nickel, zinc and aluminium. Our product range is extensive and carries a number of local manufactured products and global brands, all on a sole agency agreement. We want to assure our customers the that not only are we totally committed to give full support to their business, but that our principles are also totally dedicated in backing our success and that of our customers. Therefore we offer a wide range of materials and equipment direct from stock and also undertake material analysis, equipment calibration and reclamation of waste solder drosses. We carry an extensive range of solder ingots, wires, pastes and associated fluxes all of which are available for immediate delivery. We also carry large stocks of soldering systems, tips and accessories. Techmet has over 20 years experience in the industrial and electronics market. Our product portfolio now covers all market sectors from commercial and telecoms through to military and space applications.
