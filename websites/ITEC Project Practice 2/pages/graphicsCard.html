GPU (Graphics Processing Unit)

AMD vs. Nvidia – the one war we all benefit from. The fierce competition between the two GPU giants drives technology forwards and costs down, very good for the end-user. Modern games and applications require an incredible amount of processing and graphics cards are the hardware components that pack the punch to power through these tasks.

When you are building a gaming rig, the most important performance driven part is the graphics card. Having a dedicated graphics card in your system makes sure that you enjoy gaming at the highest level possible. With the evolution of technology, graphics cards are becoming more affordable, consume less power and deliver more performance.
