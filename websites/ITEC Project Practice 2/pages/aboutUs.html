About Us

The end is only the begining

Rebel Tech, one of South Africa’s specialist computer hardware retailers for over 13 years, has announced that it is closing down

The company’s founder, Rune Ravnsborg said “Unfortunately, due to the COVID-19 situation and its subsequent challenges, the cards that were dealt were too much for Rebel Tech to overcome,”

Who is RebelGPU

Due to the closing of Rebel Tech there has been many sadden people that long for their services. We at RebelGPU decided to make our services public but with a twist,we want to help people that are new to the PC gaming genre that are not certain on the graphics card they will need to play their most desired games while making the best decision based on thier wallets.

Our Aim
